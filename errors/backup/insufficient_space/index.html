Insufficient backup space

507

backup.insufficient_space

There is not enough disk space to complete this backup.

Common causes

  • Backup volume is nearly full.
  • Retention policy is keeping more snapshots than the volume can hold.

How to resolve

  • Prune old backups.
  • Grow the backup volume.
