Payment required

402

billing.payment_required

A valid payment method is required to complete this operation.

Common causes

  • Subscription expired or was cancelled.
  • Payment method is missing or declined.
  • Operation is on a paid plan and the tenant is on a free plan.

How to resolve

  • Update the payment method in the billing portal.
  • Upgrade to a plan that includes this feature.
