Required field missing

400

validation.field_required

A field marked as required was not present or was empty.

Common causes

  • The client omitted the property from the JSON body.
  • The property was sent as `null` or an empty string.

How to resolve

  • Send the field with a non-empty value.
  • Check the OpenAPI schema to confirm which fields are required for the endpoint.
