Errors  /  Scraping

Unknown job

404

scraping.unknown_job

The referenced scraping job does not exist.

Common causes

  • Job id is wrong.
  • Job was purged by retention policy.

How to resolve

  • List active jobs and use a valid id.
  • Re-create the job if needed.
