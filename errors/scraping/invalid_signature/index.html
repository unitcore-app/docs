Errors  /  Scraping

Invalid signature

401

scraping.invalid_signature

The scraping payload signature did not verify.

Common causes

  • Signing secret rotated and the client is using the old one.
  • Payload was modified after signing.

How to resolve

  • Re-fetch the current signing secret and re-sign the payload.
  • Sign over the exact bytes that are sent on the wire.
