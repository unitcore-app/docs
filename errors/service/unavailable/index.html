Service unavailable

503

service.unavailable

The service is temporarily unavailable. Please try again later.

Common causes

  • A downstream dependency (database, cache, queue) is unreachable.
  • The service is starting up or shutting down.
  • Rate limiting / circuit breaker tripped.

How to resolve

  • Retry with exponential backoff.
  • Check the service status page or operator dashboards.
