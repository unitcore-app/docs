Too many connections

503

service.too_many_connections

The service is at its connection limit and cannot accept this request right now.

Common causes

  • Connection pool exhausted under load.
  • A client is leaking connections.

How to resolve

  • Retry with exponential backoff.
  • Operator: investigate clients holding connections open.
