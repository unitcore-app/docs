Registration disabled

403

auth.registration_disabled

Self-service registration is not enabled for this site.

Common causes

  • The site has `allowRegistration = false` in its identity settings.
  • Registration was disabled by a Hub operator.

How to resolve

  • Enable registration in site identity settings, or ask an administrator to create the account manually.
