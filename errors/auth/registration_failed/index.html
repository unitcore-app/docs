Errors  /  Authentication & authorization

Registration failed

400

auth.registration_failed

Could not complete registration. Please review your details and try again.

Common causes

  • Email already in use.
  • Password did not meet complexity policy.
  • Required profile fields missing.

How to resolve

  • Inspect the `errors` array on the response for the specific reason.
  • Adjust the registration payload and resubmit.
