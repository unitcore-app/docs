Account locked

423

auth.account_locked

This account is locked. Please contact support.

Common causes

  • Too many failed sign-in attempts.
  • Account locked by an administrator.
  • Suspicious activity triggered an automatic lockout.

How to resolve

  • Wait for the lockout window to expire, or contact your administrator to unlock the account.
  • Hub operators can unlock from `Hub → Users → <user> → Unlock`.
